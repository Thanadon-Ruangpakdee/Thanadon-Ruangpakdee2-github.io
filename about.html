Got a power by the spider

In the shadows of a sprawling metropolis, where the ordinary and the extraordinary intersect, lies the tale of an unassuming individual who gains an incredible gift—one that is both a blessing and a curse. This transformation is not the result of a scientific experiment gone wrong or a genetic anomaly, but rather an ancient and mystical encounter with a spider of legend.

Come to protect everyone

As Miles Morales embraces their newfound powers bestowed by the mystical spider, a profound sense of responsibility begins to stir within them. This is not merely about personal gain or the thrill of adventure; it is about something much greater—the duty to protect those who cannot protect themselves.
